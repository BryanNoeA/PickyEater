Picky Eater — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 7 July 2026

Picky Eater (“the app”, “we”, “us”) is developed by Bryan Almejo (Clearforge Software). This policy explains what data the app handles. The short version: we do not collect, store, transmit, or sell any personal information about you. The app has no user accounts and no server of our own.

Information we collect

None. We operate no backend and receive no data from the app. Specifically:

  • No account. The app does not require or offer sign-in. We do not collect your name, email, or any identifier.
  • No analytics or tracking. The app contains no analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs, and does not use the Advertising Identifier (IDFA). We do not track you across apps or websites.

Location

If you use the premium “nearby restaurants” feature, the app requests when-in-use location access. Your location is used only on your device to search for nearby restaurants through Apple’s MapKit local search. Your coordinates are never sent to us, never stored, and never shared with any third party by us. You can decline or revoke this permission at any time in iOS Settings; the rest of the app continues to work without it.

Purchases

Picky Eater offers a one-time in-app purchase (Premium). All payment processing is handled entirely by Apple through the App Store and StoreKit. We never see or receive your payment details. Your purchase status is validated on-device by Apple’s StoreKit and syncs across your own devices via your Apple ID. Apple’s handling of your purchase is governed by Apple’s Privacy Policy.

Data stored on your device

Your spin history and app preferences are stored locally on your device only (via Apple’s SwiftData and standard app storage). This data never leaves your device and is removed when you delete the app. You can clear your spin history at any time from within the app.

Children’s privacy

The app does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction). It is safe for general audiences.

Your rights (GDPR / UK GDPR)

Because we do not collect or process any personal data about you, there is no personal data for us to access, correct, export, or erase. Any location use happens on your device under your control. If you are in the EEA or UK and have questions, contact us using the details below.

Your rights (California / CCPA & CPRA)

We do not collect, sell, or share personal information as defined by the CCPA/CPRA. There is therefore nothing to opt out of. California residents retain all rights under those laws; contact us with any questions.

Third parties

We do not share data with third parties because we do not collect any. The only third party involved is Apple, which provides the App Store, in-app purchases, and MapKit services you interact with directly and under Apple’s own privacy terms.

Changes to this policy

If the app’s data practices change in a future version (for example, if an optional cloud feature is added), we will update this policy and revise the “Last updated” date above before that version ships.
